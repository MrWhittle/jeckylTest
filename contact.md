## Get In Touch {#contact-form-section}

### Inquiry Form

Since this is a static Jekyll site hosted on GitHub Pages, you can use one of these methods to submit inquiries:

**Option 1: Direct Email**

Send an email to [{{ site.email }}](mailto:{{ site.email }}) with:
- Your name
- Your email address
- Company name (if applicable)
- Project description
- Budget range (if applicable)
- Preferred timeline

**Option 2: Use a Form Service**

To add a contact form to this static site, you can integrate:
- **Formspree** - No backend required, free tier available
- **Netlify Forms** - If hosted on Netlify
- **Basin** - Simple form backend service
- **Getform** - Form backend and email notifications

### Quick Response Checklist

To help us respond faster, please include:

- ✓ Clear description of your project or inquiry
- ✓ Your current situation or challenge
- ✓ Your goals or desired outcomes
- ✓ Budget range (helps us suggest solutions within your means)
- ✓ Timeline or deadline
- ✓ Best contact method and time to reach you

## Frequently Asked Questions {#faq-section}

### Pricing & Quotes

**Q: Do you provide free quotes?**

A: Yes! We provide free initial consultations and custom quotes. The quote will be based on the scope, complexity, and timeline of your project.

**Q: What's your pricing starting point?**

A: Projects vary widely, but most web design and development projects start at $3,000 and scale based on complexity. We offer packages for different budgets.

**Q: Do you offer payment plans?**

A: Yes, we offer flexible payment plans for larger projects, typically split into milestone-based payments.

---

### Services & Timeline

**Q: How long does a typical project take?**

A: Timelines depend on project scope:
- Simple website: 2-4 weeks
- Custom web app: 2-3 months
- Complex platform: 3-6+ months

**Q: Can you work with my existing website?**

A: Absolutely! We can redesign, rebuild, or enhance existing websites. We'll review your current site and provide recommendations.

**Q: What's your process?**

A: Our typical process includes discovery, planning, design, development, testing, and deployment. We keep you involved throughout.

---

### Technical Questions

**Q: Do you use WordPress?**

A: We work with various platforms including WordPress, custom builds, Jekyll, and static site generators depending on your needs and our recommendation.

**Q: Can you help with SEO?**

A: Yes! We implement SEO best practices during development and can provide ongoing SEO strategy consultation.

**Q: What about maintenance after launch?**

A: We offer several maintenance and support packages to keep your site secure, updated, and performing optimally.

**Q: Is my data secure?**

A: Security is paramount. We implement SSL certificates, regular security audits, secure databases, and follow industry best practices.

---

### Ongoing Relations

**Q: Can we work on multiple projects together?**

A: Of course! Many of our clients work with us on multiple projects. We offer package deals and long-term retainer options.

**Q: Do you offer retainer services?**

A: Yes, we offer monthly retainer packages that include ongoing support, updates, and maintenance tailored to your needs.

**Q: How do you handle changes during the project?**

A: We use a change request process to track scope changes and their impact on timeline and budget. We keep you informed every step of the way.
